About Me

I am a PhD student at the University of California, Merced, and a member of the Mi³ Laboratory under Prof. Ross Greer. My research focuses on autonomous vehicles, specifically in vision and language integration for driving scene safety.

With a background spanning NASA, Army DEVCOM, and data science for tribal epidemiology, I enjoy tackling complex problems that combine robotics, machine learning, and real-world impact.

Skills: Python, C/C++, SQL, ROS, SLAM, PyTorch, TensorFlow, OpenCV, Scikit-learn.
