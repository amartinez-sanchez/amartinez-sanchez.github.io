01 / About

Who I Am

I am a PhD student in Electrical Engineering & Computer Science at the University of California, Merced, and a member of the Mi³ Laboratory (Merced Intelligent & Interactive Imaging Lab) under Prof. Ross Greer. My research sits at the intersection of autonomous driving, computer vision, and natural language processing.

Prior to my PhD, I earned both a BS and MS in Computer Science (and a BS in Computer Information Systems with a Minor in Business Administration) from California State University, Chico. I have hands-on research experience at NASA Glenn Research Center and Army DEVCOM, and I am fluent in both English and Spanish.

Beyond research, I bring a unique background in management across restaurant, janitorial, and real estate industries — grounding my technical work with practical leadership and operational discipline.
